Welcome!

I am currently a postdoctoral research fellow in the Chinese Language Processing Group in the Computer Science Department at Brandeis University.

My main research interest is in theoretical generative syntax, its interface with morphology and semantics, as well as its application in computational linguistics.

I received my Ph.D. from the Department of Linguistics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison in December 2013. My advisors were Prof. Yafei Li and Prof. Muvert Enc.

I received my M.A. in Chinese Linguistics and B.A. in Chinese Language and Literature from the Department of Chinese Language and Literature at Peking University.
